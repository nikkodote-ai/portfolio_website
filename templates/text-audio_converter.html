Powered by AWS Polly and AWS S3. LISTEN to your Powerpoint on the go.
This app converts Powerpoint files (.pptx / .pptm) to audio which opens up a lot of possibilities when it comes to learning, studying, preparing for presentations, or just for some background noise. Is uses AI services by Amazon AWS which gives you the choice to choose standard voices or with more natural sounding voice via "neural" engine.
